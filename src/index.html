Get to Know Me

My name is Zach Butler. I am a Software Development Engineer working at Amazon within Amazon Web Services on the Simple Email Service (SES) team in Seattle. I've been developing software since my freshman year of high school, having always been fascinated by the challenge of building a great software project that consistently meets high standards of both customer experience and code quality. Through several years of constant learning and refinement of my skills and processes, I believe I consistently demonstrate my high software engineering abilities.

I am excited to build a career in a field that affects such a wide spectrum of society. Software has the ability to improve, simplify, accelerate, and refine existing processes within our daily lives, as is proven by the work I and my colleagues do on the massive (and forever growing) cloud computing platform that is AWS. So that I can have as large a positive impact I can, I block off a few hours each day to be constantly learning about the latest in software development, technology, industry breakthroughs, and whatever other information is relevant to my work.

My contact info and availability can be found at the bottom of the page. You will also find a brief overview of my resume (with a PDF link to the professional full version). My LinkedIn and GitHub profiles can be found in the navigation bar at the top of the screen.
